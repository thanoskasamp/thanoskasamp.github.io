Short Bio

Geospatial Professional

Hello, i am Athanasios Kasampalis, born in Thessaloniki, Greece in 1995. My passion for the natural world led me to pursue a degree in Forestry and Environmental Sciences at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, where I am now completing my MSc in Ecology and Conservation Biology.

This academic path, combined with my strong interest in informatics and computer programming, has led me to specialize in geospatial technologies. I have shifted my focus to the technical aspects of handling large datasets, improving database management, and developing robust mapping solutions using open source tools. My expertise lies in deploying these technologies to streamline data processes and improve the efficiency and accuracy of geospatial analysis.
